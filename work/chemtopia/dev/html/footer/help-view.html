Help Integration or Closing of Overlapped Accounts

Integration or Closing of Overlapped Accounts

Sometimes, user may have multiple number of accounts on Chemtopia.
If you receive an email notifying that your email address is used by other user, then it means you have multiple number of email accounts. That might happen when one user is using several email addresses.
When you want to integrate multiple number of email accounts into one:
  1. Log on (move onto the login page) to the account to be closed.
  2. Move to the right upper (Notification) on homepage and select ‘account closing’ of setting item.
    *Please close the account following the instruction.
  3. Log on to the remaining account after closing other accounts.
If you shut down an account, the profile will be permanently deleted, and you cannot check your Chemtopia information on Chemtopia website. Please note the following things when closing the account.
  • If you shut down your account, all related data will be deleted.
  • Your profile will not be disclosed on Chemtopia website.
  • Depending on data collection and management method of the site, your profile may appear on search result on Naver, Google and Yahoo, etc. for some time even after you close the account.
  • Tips: Process that profile appears on search site
Profile misrepresented in search result
Any information appeared on Google, Naver and Yahoo has been collected and updated in its own search engine. The point of time when your information is updated or deleted is determined by each search engine’s updating schedule.
*Chemtopia account will be shut down immediately after you close it. However, it may take up to 14 days before your public profile is deleted from Chemtopia. If any of your information is misrepresented in the search site, then please contact the concerned site and request deletion.
For information about how to delete your information on Google site, please access the following website. http://support.google.com/webmasters/bin/answer.py?answer=1663691
You may have detailed information about how to change the method of disclosing your profile on the public web search site.
  • Any and all information that you upload on Chemtopia will be deleted.
If you want to shut down the account, please move to membership page.

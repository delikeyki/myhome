Blocking or Unblocking the Follower

Blocking Follower

  1. You may check the number of followers and the list in My page customer information.
  2. If you click the follower, the follower list will pop up, and if you select unfollow in the follower lists, then the selected follower will be unfollowed, and the button will be immediately returned to Follow.
  3. If you want to revoke the unfollower, please click Follow button.
